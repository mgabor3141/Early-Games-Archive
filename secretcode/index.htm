The Secret Code

Your goal is to guess the pattern behind the ?s at the top. Each row represents one guess. The fewer the guesses, the better. Sometimes it's sheer luck, sometimes it's hardcore logic. You never know.

After a guess, you get hints in the form of dots to the right of your guess. Each • represents a color that is present in both your guess and the secret code, but is in the wrong place. •s stand for exact matches. The number of dots help you make better guesses. The order of the dots however, does not.

Beware: The Secret Code may contain more of the same color!

To start a new game, refresh the page!

Protip: Instead of clicking the color boxes on the right, you can also use the number keys (1-8), Enter and Backspace to enter your guesses faster!

Good Luck and Have Fun! :)
